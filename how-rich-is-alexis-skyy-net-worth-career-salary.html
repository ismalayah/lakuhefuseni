Alexis Skyy’s Net worth.

The Family Reunion: Love & Hip Hop Edition star has starred as a recurrent cast member on Love & Hip Hop’s Atlanta, Hollywood, and New York editions. The Dominican-Jamaican model, socialite, and rapper hails from Long Island, New York, and used to be a stripper before becoming famous as Fetty Wap’s girlfriend. The reality star’s massive net worth stems partly from her stint on the VH1 program. While she has yet to emerge as a prominent cast member of the program, her time there has been noteworthy, keeping fans interested.

She founded the Belle Sky Spa in Atlanta in 2016. Skyy was first featured on Love & Hip Hop Atlanta in 2016, then in the Hollywood part in 2017. She was featured on Love & Hip Hop New York from 2018 to 2019. The reality personality is well-known for her feuds, both online and offline.

Alexis Skyy’s Love & Hip Hop journey

In season five, the Family Reunion: Love & Hip Hop Edition star debuted in the Atlanta part of the show while posing for Tammy Rivera’s swimwear line. She then featured as Jessica Dime’s date in season 6. Her participation in the Hollywood series brought her a lot of recognition, as her conflict with Masika Kalysha became the show’s major emphasis. Skyy is now past the conflict, but when it occurred, all hell broke loose.

Masika, Moniece, and Misster Ray were on one side of the conflict, while Skyy, Hazel, Nikki, Zell, and Lyrica were on the other. The two had had a quarrel for years, but it all started on Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood when Alexis claimed that Masika became pregnant with Fetty Wap while the two were still in a relationship.

The season also included her romance with Solo Lucci, although Alexis had a falling out with Sara Scott, his child’s mother. After signing a record contract with Shotti, the prospective Family Reunion: Love & Hip Hop Edition star eventually starred in season 9 of Love & Hip Hop: New York.
