Mom (Japanese: おかあさん mother) is the mother of the player in Pokémon Scarlet and Violet.

In the core series games

Mom's appearance is dependent on which look the player chooses for themselves at the start of the game. She is a calm, friendly woman who lives in Cabo Poco with her child and pet Skwovet, having just moved to Paldea from another region. When the player is enrolled in Naranja AcademyS/Uva AcademyV, she helps them prepare for their first day of their studies and meets the academy's director, Clavell. She gives the player a Sandwich and five Potions at Cabo Poco after the player defeats Nemona for the first time. Later on, she can give the player sandwich recipes after they embark on their independent study.
