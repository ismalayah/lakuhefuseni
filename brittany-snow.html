Brittany Anne Snow (born March 9, 1986) is an American actress known for her work in film and television. She gained recognition for her role on the CBS soap opera Guiding Light (1998–2001), earning a Young Artist Award and multiple nominations. Snow then starred in the NBC drama series American Dreams (2002–2005), receiving additional award nominations. She has appeared in various films, including The Pacifier (2005), John Tucker Must Die (2006), Hairspray (2007), and the Pitch Perfect film series (2012, 2015, 2017). Snow has also made television appearances on Harry's Law (2011) and Almost Family (2019–2020). In addition to her acting career, she co-founded the Love Is Loud movement. Snow recently made her directorial debut with the film Parachute, premiering in 2023.
