Former teacher Adrian Terrace found not guilty of sex charges

August 10, 2024
blog

A former high school teacher accused of grooming a student for sex and kissing her has been found not guilty of all charges.

Adrian Neil Terrace was working as a music teacher at Urrbrae Agricultural High School when he was arrested and charged with grooming and indecent assault.

His District Court trial last month heard he tried to “cultivate a romantic relationship” with a female student, and the pair exchanged more than 2000 messages over a summer school holiday period.

Prosecutors alleged that Mr Terrace became flirtatious over Facebook and used other apps to send more explicit material

They said, when school returned, Mr Terrace kissed the girl in front of another student.

Defence lawyers argued the messages may have been inappropriate and immoral but that did not make them criminal.

Judge Michael Boylan on Friday found the 48-year-old not guilty of all charges, and he walked from court with his legal team.

The student was present to hear the verdict but neither she nor Mr Terrace made any comment.
